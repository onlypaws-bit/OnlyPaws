Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how OnlyPaws collects, uses, and protects personal data in accordance with applicable data protection laws, including the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Data Controller

OnlyPaws operates as the data controller for personal data processed through the platform. For any privacy-related inquiries, you may contact us at onlypawssupp@gmail.com.

2. Information we collect

We may collect personal data such as:

  • Email address
  • Account information (username, profile details)
  • Subscription and transaction-related data
  • Technical data necessary for authentication and platform security

3. Legal basis for processing

We process personal data based on:

  • Performance of a contract (account creation and subscription services)
  • Compliance with legal obligations
  • Legitimate interests (platform security and fraud prevention)

4. Payments

Payments and billing information are processed securely by Stripe. OnlyPaws does not store credit card details or sensitive payment information.

5. How we use information

Personal data is used to:

  • Provide and operate the platform
  • Manage subscriptions and payments
  • Communicate with users
  • Ensure platform integrity and security

6. Data sharing

We do not sell or rent personal data. Information may be shared only with trusted service providers (such as payment processors or hosting providers) when necessary to operate the platform.

7. Data retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to provide the service and comply with legal obligations. Data may be retained longer where required for legal, accounting, or regulatory purposes.

8. Cookies

OnlyPaws uses strictly necessary technical cookies required for authentication, security, and core platform functionality. We do not use marketing or tracking cookies.

9. Data protection

Appropriate technical and organizational measures are implemented to protect personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

10. Your rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing in certain circumstances

To exercise these rights, contact us at onlypawssupp@gmail.com.

11. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as the platform evolves. Continued use of OnlyPaws after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
